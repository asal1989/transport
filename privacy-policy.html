Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

1. Introduction

TNSTC பென்ஷன் கணக்கீடு ("the App") is a retirement benefits calculator for Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation (TNSTC/SETC/MTC) employees. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal data. The App is a standalone calculator that works entirely on your device. All calculations are performed locally and no data is sent to any server.

The App does not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial data
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics

3. Data Storage

The App uses your device's session storage only to maintain your login session while the app is open. This data is automatically cleared when you close the app or log out. No data is stored permanently or transmitted anywhere.

4. Internet Access

The App loads Google Fonts for Tamil text display. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for adults (government employees and retirees). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
